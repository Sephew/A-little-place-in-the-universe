# 11 — Grove: past-trees list + travel

*Tier: Post-MVP · Stories: 37.*

## What to build

A list of the user's **past (archived) Trees** in the quiet panel; selecting one **travels into** that Tree (re-rendered from its deterministic recipe) to **re-read its Entries, read-only**. Faint background silhouettes and travel-to-a-light navigation stay deferred.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] The quiet panel lists the user's archived Trees.
- [ ] Selecting one renders that Tree from its recipe and lists its Entries, read-only (no writing, no hiding).
- [ ] Returning brings you back to the current Tree.
